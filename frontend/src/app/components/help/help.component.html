Help

1. How to Create an Account

For Students:

Click the "sign-up" toggle and enter your first and last name, ID number, email, and phone number. Create a password and click "Submit". Click the "Log-in" toggle and sign in with your email and password.


To View FAQs

Select FAQs from the menu to see if a question or complaint you have has previously been addressed.


2. To Submit a Complaint

Click the menu button in the top left corner, then click "Submit a Complaint"


3. To View Your Complaint Status

Select "Complaint Status" from the menu.


4. For SSO, CSC, and Admin:

Click the link at the bottom of the sign-up window that says "for administration. Log in with the correct email and password for your position and click "Submit"
